The Lightweight Mat 5mm
* Grid-textured top layer adds light grip in low-sweat, low-heat practices. Pair with The Towel for optimal performance in sweaty or high-heat practices
* The top layer of this mat is made using recycled PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral), a resin used in safety glass such as windshields that would otherwise end up as waste
* Lightweight foam base layer for added cushion
* Closed cell design keeps sweat and other moisture out
* Stain resistant and easy to clean
